To get to the bedrock server, in minecraft windows 10 edition or pocket edition, go to the servers tab and scroll down to add new. Make sure the port is 19132 and in the ip section type globalcraft.cloudwatch.net.


About

Just like the java server, the bedrock server is a survival world where players are encouraged to work together towards their goals, once all bosses are slain, everyone will vote to either keep playing for another week, or have the map switched.
